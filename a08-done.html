A Registry Officer will review your submission and determine whether they can file the document(s).

  Accepted documents with no filing fee
If your document(s) comply with the Rules and formatting standards, and do not require payment of a filing fee:

  1. The Registry will file your document(s).
    • Your documents will be deemed as filed on the date and time of your E-Filing document submission in the Eastern time (ET) zone; unless you submitted on a Saturday, Sunday, or holiday. If you submitted on a weekend or holiday, they will be deemed as filed on the next business day.
  2. You (or your contact) will receive an e-mail confirming that your document(s) have been filed. The email will contain a link to the e-filed document(s), which will be stamped electronically.

  Accepted documents with a filing fee
If your document(s) comply with the Rules and formatting standards, but are on the list of documents that require a filing fee:

  1. The Registry will send you a request for online payment by email with the amount to be paid.
  2. You can pay online using the link provided in the email, or call the Registry to request a different means of payment.
  3. Once your payment is received, you will receive an e-mail confirming that your document(s) have been filed. The email will contain a link to the e-filed document(s), which will be stamped electronically.
    • Your documents will be deemed as filed on the date and time of your E-Filing document submission in the Eastern time (ET) zone; unless you submitted on a Saturday, Sunday, or holiday. If you submitted on a weekend or holiday, they will be deemed as filed on the next business day.

  Returned documents
If your document(s) do not comply with the formatting standards or other rules:

  1. The Registry will send you an e-mail to inform you of the filing status and provide instructions.
  2. After you re-submit the document(s) through the E-Filing application, they will be reviewed again.
  3. If you do not re-submit your document(s) by the deadline in the e-mail, they will be refused for filing. You will receive a follow-up email to inform you of the rejection and the reason.
