    You will receive an initial e-mail confirmation of your submission, with the confirmation number above.

    A Registry Officer will review your submission and determine whether they can file the document(s).

      Accepted document(s) with no filing fee
    If your document(s) comply with the Rules and formatting standards, and do not require payment of a filing fee:

    1. The Registry will file your document(s).
      • Your document(s) will be deemed as filed on the date and time of your E-Filing document submission in the Eastern time (ET) zone; unless you submitted on a Saturday, Sunday, or holiday. If you submitted on a weekend or holiday, they will be deemed as filed on the next business day.
    2. You will receive an e-mail verifying that your document(s) have been filed, with the case file number and a link to the stamped document(s).

      Accepted document(s) with a filing fee
    If your document(s) comply with the Rules and formatting standards, but require a filing fee in Tariff A or IMM Rule 23

  • The Registry will send you an e-mail with a request for online payment.
  • You can pay online using the link provided in the email, or call the Registry to request a different means of payment.
  • Once your payment is received, you will receive an e-mail verifying that your document(s) have been filed, with the case file number and a link to the stamped document(s).
    • Your document(s) will be deemed as filed on the date and time of your E-Filing document submission in the Eastern time (ET) zone; unless you submitted on a Saturday, Sunday, or holiday. If you submitted on a weekend or holiday, they will be deemed as filed on the next business day.

    •   Returned document(s)
    If your document(s) do not comply with the formatting standards or other rules:

    1. The Registry will send you an e-mail to inform you of the filing status and provide instructions.
    2. After you re-submit the document(s) through the E-Filing application, they will be reviewed again.
    3. If you do not re-submit your document(s), they will be refused for filing. The Registry will send you an e-mail to inform you of the rejection and the reason.
